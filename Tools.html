BMI Calculator

BMI Calculator

BMI, Body Mass Index, is used to screen for weight categories that may have a higher risk for health problems. It is an indicator of the nutritional status ranging from underweight to obesity, not a measure of body fat percentage or the health of an individual.

BMI Table

  • Below 18.5 Underweight
  • 18.5–24.9 > Normal weight
  • 25.0–29.9 > Overweight
  • 30.0–34.9 > Class I obesity
  • 35.0–39.9 > Class II obesity
  • 40 > Class III obesity
